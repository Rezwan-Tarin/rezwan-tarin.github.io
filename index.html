Summary

I'm an Information Security Specialist with 3+ years of hands-on enterprise security experience. I got my start in finance at TD Bank, where three years of managing sensitive client information under regulatory pressure taught me that real security is about process and trust, not just technology.

That foundation led me to cybersecurity. After completing a Computer Programming Analyst diploma at George Brown College, I joined Teranet Inc. as an Information Security Specialist. Over the past 3+ years I've rolled out Zero Trust, migrated WAF infrastructure for 20+ applications, replaced the SIEM and EDR stack, built AI governance standards, and pushed phishing awareness from 88% to 98%.
